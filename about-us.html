This is Dixie's Doggy Daycare

Dixie's is a small-scale daycare centre situated on the outskirts Swindon. There are several access roads which make it easy for you to get here by car. Several parks, where we spend a lot of time, are within walking distance.
We have a limit of maximum fifteen enrolled dogs. Therefore the staff knows all the dogs personalities and your dog will be surrounded by professionals who can meet it's special needs.

Safety is Dixie's highest priority. You should feel confident that we do our best at all times, to ensure your dog's safety. The staff who work at Dixie's all have many years experience of caring for dogs.
